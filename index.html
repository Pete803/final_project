GROUP ONE FINAL PROJECT

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

Mortgage Calculator

Created a 'pre-qualifying' online tool where customers can enter income, requested loan amount, loan term, interest rate, home value, property tax, PMI and home insurance and HOA fees, if any.

Research

Explore the criteria for credit worthiness of mortgage applicant to apply to our credit model

Heroku

Successfully create and deploy and responsive HTML site using Heroku.

Tableau

Created interactive maps using Tableau

Credit Model

Using python and machine learning we created and deployed a credit model based on mock data. We used 1500 records to train the model and 500 records to test.  Our trained model predicted a 20% approval rate – which matched the true approval rate of our applicants.

Mockaroo Data

We utilized a test data generator and API mocking tool. Mockaroo let our team create custom datasets to test and demo our credit model.

SUMMARY

Credit risk is an important topic in the field of finance because banks and other financial institutions heavily invest in reducing their credit risk.

Poor credit score indicates that a customer has a higher probability of ​defaulting on a loan. 

One of the main drivers behind the global financial crisis of 2008 was due to mortgage loans given to customers with poor credit scores.
